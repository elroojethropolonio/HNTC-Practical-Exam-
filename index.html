Project creation highlight compilation

This website contains a record of highlighted events during the creation of various projects in Social Sciences and English in the first quarter

(Along with some emojis to lighten the mood)



1st quarter (Social Sciences)

1. Sleep deprivation 😴

Despite multiple class suspensions, we still ended up rushing the research base that we were supposed to submit online. Before we knew it, we ended up staying up till 2 in the morning.

2. Shenanigans 😜

While making the script through a shared doc, we sorta procrastinated and ended up joking around for hours before actually starting the script.

3. Pre-Practice 🙃

Our practice was very delayed because apparently a groupmate had to chase down a person for rent money.

4. Recording 🎥

After practicing, we were confident enough to start recording, using my computer, I recorded 37 clips in total (not including successful ones) and they all amounted to 28 gigabytes on my hardrive.

5. Editing ✂

I had to sort through and piece together about 13 clips to make the raw podcast. I then added effects and spent about an hour properly timing the background beat drop with key words throughout the podcast.





2nd quarter (English)

1. Impromptu 😂

I spoke gibberish for 2 minutes straight and people thought I was saying something complicated.

2. Reliable research 📚

We were taught the different sources information and how to tell if that information was reliable. We were then told to research on the "positive and negative effects of social media"

3. Debate 🗣

The class was divided into two groups (Pro and Con), each group compiled Information about the subject and debated using it.

4. Arguementative essay 📖

My essay was handwritten and my handwriting isnt necessarily the best so I had difficulty reading my own essay. I ended up having to rewrite half my essay in pen so it was more readable and I had to memorize the rest just before my turn, I got really lucky here because there were many people who presented before I did.
